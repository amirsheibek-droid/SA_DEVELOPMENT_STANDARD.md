Common Questions

Frequently asked questions.

How much does a website cost?

Every build is quoted individually at your free consultation, and the cost is fixed in writing before any work begins. Your only ongoing costs are your domain and business email, passed on at cost — hosting is provided free.

Do I see anything before I pay?

Yes. Every package begins with a complimentary working mock-up of your site, so you can review the direction before committing to anything.

Do you build mobile applications?

Yes. Alongside website design, we develop mobile applications for specialist niches — current products in development include AXreporter, an accident reporting application, and Contract Analyser, an intelligent contract review application.

Is SEO included?

Every website is built with SEO support. The Launch package includes foundational SEO setup, and the Signature package adds full on-page SEO with analytics.

Am I tied into a contract?

No. The care plan is optional, billed in advance and can be cancelled at any time without penalty. You buy and own your domain in your own name.

Privacy Policy

This website does not store any personal data. It's a static site with no database and no server-side processing.

The consultation form sends your enquiry directly by email — it is not saved to any database. We only use the details you send us to reply to your enquiry.

We don't use analytics, tracking scripts, or third-party advertising on this site.

Cookies Policy

Essential storage. We use strictly necessary browser storage only: a single record that keeps the site working and remembers your consent choice. Under UK GDPR and PECR, strictly necessary storage does not require consent.

Optional cookies. We do not currently use any analytics, tracking or advertising cookies. If that ever changes, they will remain off until you opt in via the cookie notice. Choosing "Reject all" refuses every optional category; only strictly necessary storage remains, including the record of your refusal so we don't ask again.

Changing your mind. Clear this site's data in your browser settings and the cookie notice will reappear, letting you choose again. Nothing you enter here is ever shared with third parties.
